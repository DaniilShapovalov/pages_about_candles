The stages of creating candles

Creating a soy candle can involve the following stages:

  1. Preparing materials: small pieces of soy wax, a wick for the candle, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials.

  2. Preparing the mold: choosing a mold for the candle and preparing it for wax filling.

  3. Preparing the wax: melting soy wax in a melting device to the required temperature. Adding fragrances and dyes if necessary.

  4. Preparing the wick: inserting the wick into the mold and securing it evenly.

  5. Preparing materials: small fragments of soy wax, candle wick, fragrances, dyes, and other additional materials.

  6. Filling the mold: pouring wax into the mold until it's completely filled. Waiting for it to solidify.
